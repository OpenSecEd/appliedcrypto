### Content

Probability distributions and entropy measures. Random numbers vs pseudo
random numbers. Practical solutions for generating and testing
randomness and their advantages and disadvantages (good and bad
designs). Physical (\"true\" and \"heuristic\") random generators,
pseudo random (\"deterministic\") generators, cryptographically strong
generators. Standards for (pseudo) random number generation.
