For some students and some bookings, being logged into KTH and into
Canvas, and having the calendar for the course selected, is not enough
to see available appointments. The next thing to try is to look for the
\"Find Appointment\" button in the calendar and click that. This seems
to work in such cases. See more details here:

[https://www.kth.se/en/student/it/learning-platforms/hitta-och-reservera-tid-i-canvas-kalender-1.1158346](https://www.kth.se/en/student/it/learning-platforms/hitta-och-reservera-tid-i-canvas-kalender-1.1158346){target="_blank"}
