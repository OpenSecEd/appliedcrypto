### Operational grading criteria

-   Overall course grade: same as LAB1, given P in INL1
-   INL1 (2.5 ECTS): P on all components
-   LAB1 (5 ECTS): E for passing all mandatory assignments, higher
    grades for additional optional higher scored (up to 2.5) assignments
    (criteria for grades of individual assignments given as rubrics
    attached to the assignments), as in the table below.

 

  ----------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------- ---------------------------- ---------------------------- ----------------------------
  Higher LAB1 grades given E of mandatory                     Optional assignments for D   Optional assignments for C   Optional assignments for B   Optional assignments for A
  ⌈∑⌉ = rounded up sum of points from optional assignments.   ⌈∑⌉ = 1                      ⌈∑⌉ = 2                      ⌈∑⌉ = 3                      ∑\>= 4

### Operational grading criteria

-   Overall course grade: weighted average of LAB and INL1, rounded down
-   INL1 (2.5 ECTS): final grade of INL1Written, given that INL1Quiz and
    INL1Oral are P.
-   LAB (5 ECTS): grades from mandatory exercises up to C, higher grades
    from optional A, B, or C-graded assignments (criteria for grades of
    individual assignments will be given as rubrics attached to the
    assignments), as in table below.

 

  -------------------------------- -- ---------------------------- ---------------------------- ---------------------------- ----------------------------
  LAB1 grade from mandatory part      Optional assignments for D   Optional assignments for C   Optional assignments for B   Optional assignments for A
  E                                   1C                           2C or 1B                     2B or 1A                     2A
  D                                   none                         1C                           1C and 1B                    1A and 1B
  C                                   none                         none                         1B                           1A  and 1C
  -------------------------------- -- ---------------------------- ---------------------------- ---------------------------- ----------------------------
