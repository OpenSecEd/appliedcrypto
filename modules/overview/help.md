## \"Any time\"

Use the
[Discussions](https://canvas.kth.se/courses/59543/discussion_topics "Discussions"){api-endpoint="https://canvas.kth.se/api/v1/courses/59543/discussion_topics"
api-returntype="[Discussion]"}
feature[](https://canvas.kth.se/courses/59543/discussion_topics "General, informal course discussion"){api-endpoint="https://canvas.kth.se/api/v1/courses/59543/discussion_topics"
api-returntype="[Discussion]"}. Post your question and you will
eventually get an answer.

## During lab sessions

To get help during the lab sessions (see the schedule linked on [Class
zoom, schedule, lab
queue](https://canvas.kth.se/courses/59543/pages/class-zoom-schedule-lab-queue "Class zoom, schedule, lab queue"){course-type="wikiPages"
published="true"
api-endpoint="https://canvas.kth.se/api/v1/courses/59543/pages/class-zoom-schedule-lab-queue"
api-returntype="Page"} for when they are), you should do the following.

Set up [Zoom](https://zoom.us/download) and host a new meeting (see
fig. [1](#fig:new)). In the meeting room, select "Participants" at the
bottom of the screen and find the "Invite" button (see
fig. [2](#fig:participants)). Then click "Copy invite link" (see
fig. [3](#fig:invite)).

Head to the [help queue](https://queue.csc.kth.se/Queue/DD2520). Use the
invite link as the location and hit "join queue" to join the queue to
wait for help (see fig. [4](#fig:queue)). When it's your turn, a tutor
will join your Zoom meeting.

## Help after the course

After the course, help within the course is limited to other students
replying in the Canvas forum. But you can get help and present
assignments during [Lab Week in
June](https://www.kth.se/social/group/labbvecka/){.inline_disabled
target="_blank"}.
